Independant output for the open hat and the closed hat

## Controls are :

 - Open hat Decay
 - Open hat Volume
 - Closed hat Decay
 - Closed hat Volume
 - White noise / Metal noise MIX
 - White noise tone
 - Metal Caracter
 - Metal Tune
 - Interaction between Open/Closed hat (if switch ON, the Closed hat will cancel the open one (like a reel one)

### Inputs

 - Open hat Trigger
 - Closed hat Trigger
 - [ ] Open hat Decay CV
 - [ ] Closed hat Decay CV
